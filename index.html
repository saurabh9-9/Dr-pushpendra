About the Doctor

Healing Through Movement

Dr. Pushpendra is a highly skilled physiotherapist with over 15 years of clinical experience in musculoskeletal, neurological, and sports rehabilitation. His evidence-based, patient-centred approach ensures that every treatment plan is tailored specifically to the individual's needs and goals.

He specialises in treating acute and chronic pain, post-surgical recovery, sports injuries, and neurological conditions — helping patients regain mobility, reduce pain, and improve their quality of life.
